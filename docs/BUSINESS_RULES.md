# Business Rules

## 1. Business Overview

### 1.1 Purpose

Courta is a Sports Center Management System designed to digitize the daily operations of a single sports center.

The system centralizes booking management, pricing, payment verification, customer management, operational reporting, and website content management into one integrated platform.

The primary objective is to reduce manual administrative work while improving booking accuracy, operational efficiency, and customer experience.

---

### 1.2 Business Model

Courta is designed for a **single business**, not a marketplace.

The system manages only one sports center, where all courts, schedules, pricing, and operational activities belong to the same business owner.

Customers interact directly with the sports center without intermediary vendors or third-party court providers.

---

### 1.3 Supported Sports

The first version supports:

- Futsal
- Volleyball

Additional sports may be added in future versions without changing the overall business workflow.

---

## 2. System Scope

Courta manages operational activities including:

- Court booking
- Payment verification
- Court management
- Pricing management
- Customer management
- Website content management
- Financial reporting
- Business analytics

The system does not include:

- Marketplace functionality
- Multi-business management
- Automatic payment gateway integration
- Tournament management
- Membership system

These features are considered future enhancements.

---

## 3. User Roles

Courta defines three user roles.

### Customer

Customers can:

- Register an account.
- Login.
- Manage their profile.
- View available courts.
- Check court schedules.
- Create bookings.
- Upload payment proof.
- View booking history.
- View booking status.

Customers cannot:

- Modify pricing.
- Verify payments.
- Manage court information.
- Access operational reports.

---

### Admin

Admins are responsible for daily operational activities.

Admins can:

- Manage bookings.
- Verify payments.
- Manage court availability.
- Manage pricing.
- Manage promotions.
- Manage website content.
- Manage customer accounts.

Admins cannot:

- Access business analytics.
- Access financial reports intended for business owners.
- Manage owner accounts.

---

### Owner

Owners have unrestricted operational access.

Owners can:

- Perform all administrator actions.
- Access business analytics.
- Access financial reports.
- View booking statistics.
- Configure operational settings.
- Manage administrator accounts.
